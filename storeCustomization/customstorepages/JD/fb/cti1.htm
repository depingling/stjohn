What is the Customer Training Institute?

 

The Customer Training Institute (CTI) is an educational program offered by JohnsonDiversey’s North American Food Group. The objective of this program is to increase an individual’s knowledge of sanitation. With recent incidences of pathogenic microorganisms in products, increasing shelf-life demands, and recalls; sanitation remains a critically important component in a plant’s operation. At JohnsonDiversey we feel it is our responsibility not only to provide the best chemicals and application systems, but to enable our customers to find solutions to sanitation problems through education and knowledge.

 
 

In Successful CIP (Clean in Place) in Food Plants students learn in JohnsonDiversey’s CIP Wet Laboratory. In this portion, participants are learning the components of a CIP system, how the systems operate and then shown troubleshooting techniques.

What is the charge for attending a session at the Customer Training Institute?

 

There is no charge for attending a session at the Customer Training Institute. In addition, JohnsonDiversey supplies the training materials and lunch.  As mentioned previously, we feel this is part of our commitment to the food industry.

 

Travel and lodging costs are the responsibility of the attendee though.  JohnsonDiversey will suggest lodging location information with a reasonable group rate.
