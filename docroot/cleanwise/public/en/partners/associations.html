American Society for Healthcare Environmental Service (ASHES) is the premier health care association for over 1,650 environmental services, housekeeping, and textile care professionals. It is the largest health care environmental services organization in the world advising companies in such areas as: healthcare environmental services/housekeeping policies and procedures, training, waste management, staffing, and more.

Building Service Contractors Association International (BSCAI) is the trade association of the building service industry aimed at advancing the building service contracting industry worldwide. The association has grown to represent a worldwide network of more than 2,500 member companies from across the US and 39 other countries, who provide cleaning, facility maintenance, and other related services to building owners and managers.

Institute of Inspection, Cleaning and Restoration Certification (IICRC) is an independent, nonprofit certification body that aims to advance communication and technical proficiency within the cleaning, inspection and restoration industry.

International Sanitary Supply Association (ISSA) is the leading international trade association for the cleaning and maintenance industry with more than 4,600 distributors, wholesalers, manufacturers, and related companies. ISSA promotes leadership in the jan/san industry by helping its members to develop the management and technological sophistication needed to ensure productivity and profitability.
