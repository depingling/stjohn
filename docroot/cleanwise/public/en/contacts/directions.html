From Points North (Manchester Airport):

  • Exit the airport and take a right at the first set of lights on to Brown Avenue.
  • Proceed to Route 293 North.
  • Go north on Route 293.
  • Take the second exit to Route 3 (Everett Turnpike).
  • Proceed on Route 3 South to Route 495 South towards Marlborough.
  • Travel about 25 miles to exit 24B (Route 20 West).
  • Follow Route 20 West through the set of lights at the St. Mary’s Credit Union.
  • Our building is the next one on the right, 33 West.
  • We are on the 4th floor.

From Points East (Boston/Logan Airport):

  • From all terminals, follow signs to Route 1A South/Sumner Tunnel.
  • Proceed through the tunnel, staying in the right lane (the toll for the tunnel is $2).
  • Turn right at the end of tunnel and follow signs for 93 South/3 South/90 West/Downtown Boston.
  • Proceed through the stoplight and follow the road to the left under the highway.
  • Due to on-going construction, your route may vary, but eventually you will be directed to a ramp onto Route 93 South.
  • Stay in the right lane of 93 South for about 1 mile.
  • Take exit 20 for the Mass Pike/All Points West.
  • Travel 28 miles.
  • Take exit 11A to Route 495 North towards Marlborough/Milford.
  • Travel 5 miles to exit 24B (Route 20 West).
  • Follow Route 20 West through the set of lights at the St. Mary’s Credit Union.
  • Our building is the next one on the right, 33 West.
  • We are on the 4th floor.

From Points South (T.F. Green Airport, Providence, RI):

  • Head west on Airport Connector Road.
  • Take the exit for Route 95 North towards Providence.
  • Travel 25 miles and take exit 6B to Route 495 North towards Worcester.
  • Travel 30 miles to exit 24B (Route 20 West).
  • Follow Route 20 West through the set of lights at the St. Mary’s Credit Union.
  • Our building is the next one on the right, 33 West.
  • We are on the 4th floor.
