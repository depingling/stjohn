The Cleanwise Solution combines our pillars of strength: technology, industry expertise, and supply chain management to quantify opportunities for cost savings and efficiencies associated with procuring jan/san products and services.

We begin by researching and understanding our customer's jan/san needs and discover opportunities and cost saving benefits associated with:

  • People
  • Process
  • Products
  • Price

Some of the areas where Cleanwise uncovers cost saving opportunities include:
  • Sourcing/Product Acquisition
  • Procurement
  • Contract Compliance
  • Inventory Control
  • Risk Management
  • Productivity Improvement (Labor/Service Costs)

As we gather information from customer interviews, facility tours, observations of existing business processes, technology, and supplier and distributor relations, Cleanwise creates a cost savings analysis based on our Value Delivery Process™.

The Value Delivery Process is the foundation to outlining and implementing our customer's deployment plan. Cleanwise uses the Value Delivery model as a live document on a quarterly basis to measure, monitor, and report the progress, cost savings, and achievements of its customers.
