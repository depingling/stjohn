CLEANWISE ANNOUNCES $5 MILLION IN ADDITIONAL FUNDING BRINGING TOTAL RAISED TO $15.25 MILLION

New Investor THK Private Equities joins Johnson Wax Professional and Insight Capital Partners.

MARLBOROUGH, Mass., April. 16, 2001 -- Cleanwise, a company dedicated to consolidating and streamlining the janitorial/sanitary (jan/san) supply chain, today announced that it has secured $5 million in additional funding bringing the total raised to $15.25 million. The Series C round includes new investor THK Private Equities, as well as previous investor Insight Capital Partners.

The company's Series A financing, in February 2000, raised $4 million and was led by Insight, with private investors also participating. Its series B financing raised $6.25 million in October 2000, with Johnson Wax Professional leading and Insight returning.

"The support we've received in this tough financing environment is a strong validation of Cleanwise's vision, business model and technology," said Keith Alsheimer, president of Cleanwise. "By automating existing industry relationships, Cleanwise enables companies in the jan/san supply chain to truly optimize their procurement and operations management processes for the first time, without requiring large investments in information technology. As a result, Cleanwise has proved to be a strong investment opportunity."

The company will use the funds to continue to build out its service infrastructure and expand sales and marketing efforts.

About Cleanwise
Cleanwise provides the technology and infrastructure to manage the complex business rules and unique supply chain requirements in the $20 billion North American janitorial and sanitary supply industry. Cleanwise streamlines and integrates activities across existing supply chain participants resulting in greater efficiency, control, and cost savings. Through its Cleanwise.com site and eStores offerings, Cleanwise provides the industry's only complete source for the procurement of jan/san products, services, training and information.

Based in Marlborough, Mass., Cleanwise was founded by jan/san industry veterans in December 1999.
