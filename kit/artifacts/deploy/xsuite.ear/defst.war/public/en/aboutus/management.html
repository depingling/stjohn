Keith Alsheimer, President
Keith has more than a decade of experience in the janitorial and sanitary supply industry, serving most recently as the vice president of marketing for the Butcher Company, a major manufacturer of industrial cleaning chemicals. Keith is widely recognized for his in-depth understanding of the contract cleaning industry, as well as health care, education and hospitality industry segments. Keith is responsible for the development of two of the industry's leading dilution control systems and holds a patent for his creation of a unique training platform for the janitorial industry. Keith served in the United States Army as a company commander and holds a bachelor's degree from Vanderbilt University.

Doug Bachelor, Chief Technology Officer
Doug brings to Cleanwise more than 19 years of experience in software development and systems integration spanning host, client/server, and Internet architecture. Before joining Cleanwise, he was Founder and CTO of SinglePoint Financial where he designed and developed advanced Internet products and infrastructure for financial services, and built and managed a team of 20 developers that earned revenues of $2 million annually. Prior to his tenure at SinglePoint Financial, Doug was the Senior Director of Data Architecture at New England Business Services. While there, he re-engineered the company OTLP, OLAP and marketing database applications and integrated ERP applications with marketing and OLAP data warehouses. Doug has also held senior positions at several organizations including: Early, Cloud & Company, Digital Tools, Project Software and others. He holds a Bachelor of Arts in chemistry from the University of Indiana, Bachelor of Science in Accounting and Data Processing from Minot State College, and a Master of Business Administration from the University of North Dakota. Doug also served in the United States Air Force.

Louise Mawhinney, Chief Financial Officer
Prior to joining Cleanwise, Louise served as the CFO of Veridiem, Inc., where she helped the software start-up secure $25 million in funding from North Bridge Venture Partners, Venrock Associates and other top-tier venture firms. She has a solid understanding of the jan/san industry, having spent six years at the Butcher Company, most recently as treasurer and chief financial officer. Louise is a certified public accountant and has built significant expertise in mergers and acquisitions while working at KPMG and Grant Thornton. She holds a master's degree from St. Andrews University in Scotland.

Board of Directors

Michael J. Bailey
Mike is the Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Commercial Markets at Johnson Wax Professional. His responsibilities include finance, mergers and acquisitions, and information technology. He serves as the chairman of the e-Business Steering Committee, with oversight for the Company's internal and external e-business efforts. Mike has a Bachelor of Arts from Western Michigan University and a Master of Business Administration from the University of Michigan.

Charles Butcher
Until September 2000 Charles was owner of the Butcher Company. The Butcher Company is a global supplier of an extensive line of institutional, commercial and industrial cleaning products, and has been one of the leading names in floor care cleaning products for over 100 years.

Bill Doyle
Bill is a Managing Director at Insight Capital Partners and brings over 15 years experience in the advanced technology and healthcare industries with a focus on B2B e-commerce. Prior to joining Insight, Bill was a member of the Consumer Pharmaceutical and Professional Group Operating Committee and VP, Licensing and Acquisitions of Johnson & Johnson. He was also a director of Johnson & Johnson Development Corporation, J&J's venture capital subsidiary. During his career, Bill acted as a consultant with McKinsey & Company and was involved in the founding of both Durel Corporation and BellGeospace, Inc. He was the first employee of American Superconductor. Bill is an Adjunct Professor of Entrepreneurship at Columbia University's Graduate School of Business Administration in New York, and holds a SB in engineering from MIT and a Master of Business Administration from Harvard Business School.

Paul McLaughlin
Paul served as President of the Butcher Company. Prior to his 22-year career at Butcher's he served as Assistant Vice President for the Malden Trust Company. Paul holds a Bachelor of Arts from Babson College with degrees in Business Administration, Economics and Accounting.
