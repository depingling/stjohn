THE UNITED STATES POSTAL SERVICE SELECTS CLEANWISE AS ONE OF THEIR CUSTODIAL PRODUCT SUPPLIERS.

Marlborough, Mass., February 2002 -- Cleanwise Inc. announced today it will provide custodial supplies to the United States Postal Service effective November 1, 2001, based on a contract awarded on August 13, 2001. The USPS awarded two four-year contracts (with 6 year renewable options), one of which went to Cleanwise a company that specializes in managing and streamlining the janitorial/sanitary needs of corporations with multiple locations.

Cleanwise Inc. will provide 40,000 USPS locations nationwide with technology, tools, industry expertise, and the ability to manage the supply chain resulting in a flexible and cost effective solution to regulate, measure, and control facility costs.

"The US Postal Service is the ideal client to benefit from the specialized services provided by Cleanwise," stated Keith Alsheimer, President of Cleanwise, Inc. "Their ability to recognize and understand the importance of technology and our ability to provide a flexible management solution strengthens this partnership. We expect to help the USPS improve buying practices, obtain best value, and reduce costs during the lifecycle of custodial products."

About Cleanwise, Inc.
Cleanwise provides optimization and management services designed to reduce janitorial and sanitary (jan/san) product procurement costs and supply chain complexities for multi location enterprises. By incorporating technology, industry expertise, and supply chain management Cleanwise streamlines and integrates activities across the entire (jan/san) procurement process resulting in greater efficiency, control, and cost savings. Cleanwise provides the industry's only complete source for procurement of jan/san products, services, training, reporting, and information. Based in Marlborough, Mass., Cleanwise was founded by jan/san industry veterans in December 1999.
