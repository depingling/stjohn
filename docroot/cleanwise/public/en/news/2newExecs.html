CLEANWISE BOLSTERS MANAGEMENT TEAM WITH TWO SIGNIFICANT EXECUTIVE HIRES

New Team Members Strengthen Cleanwise's Business and Technology Expertise and Bring International Sales Experience

Marlborough, Mass., March 24, 2001 -- Cleanwise, a company dedicated to consolidating and streamlining the janitorial/sanitary (jan/san) supply chain, today announced the hires of Doug Bachelor, chief technology officer, and Alan Facey, vice president of worldwide field operations. These two management hires further solidify Cleanwise's position as a leading provider of online products, services, and expertise for the jan/san industry.

As chief technology officer, Bachelor is responsible for Cleanwise's engineering, development, and technical operations. He brings more than 19 years of experience in software development and systems integration spanning host, client/server, and Internet architecture. Before joining Cleanwise, he was founder and CTO of SinglePoint Financial where he designed and developed advanced Internet products and infrastructure for financial services, and built and managed a team of 20 developers. Prior to his tenure at SinglePoint Financial, Bachelor was the senior director of data architecture at New England Business Services. While there, he re-engineered the company OTLP, OLAP and marketing database applications and integrated ERP applications with marketing and OLAP data warehouses.

Bachelor has also held senior positions at several organizations including: Early, Cloud & Company, Digital Tools, Project Software and others. He holds a B.A. in chemistry from University of Indiana, B.S. in Accounting and Data Processing from Minot State College, and an M.B.A. from the University of North Dakota. Bachelor also served in the United States Air Force.

In his role as executive vice president of worldwide field operations, Facey will guarantee the success of all customer deployments, as well as overseeing the sales, marketing, and professional services groups. Facey has more than twenty years experience working in various roles around the globe. Most recently, Facey was responsible for sales, marketing, professional services, customer relationship management, and a staff of 350 people as vice president of North American field operations for Progress Software. He began as the vice president of field operations for Europe, Middle East and Africa, based in the Netherlands, and overseeing revenue growth of more than 30% annually during his tenure.

Prior to Progress Software, Facey held senior positions in marketing, sales, professional services and operations with Ballast Nedam, Holistic Systems, and Metier Management Systems. He holds a Bachelor of Science from London University.

"Doug and Alan nicely round-out the Cleanwise plan, by perfectly complementing our existing management team," said Keith Alsheimer, president. "We look forward to benefiting from their extensive experience in international business and technology as they help us improve and streamline the jan/san supply chain."

About Cleanwise
Cleanwise provides the technology and infrastructure to manage the complex business rules and unique supply chain requirements in the $20 billion North American janitorial and sanitary supply industry. Cleanwise streamlines and integrates activities across existing supply chain participants resulting in greater efficiency, control, and cost savings. Through its Cleanwise.com site and eStores offerings, Cleanwise provides the industry's only complete source for the procurement of jan/san products, services, training and information.

Based in Marlborough, Mass., Cleanwise was founded by jan/san industry veterans in December 1999.
